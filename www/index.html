                            OAKDALE         

            Well here we go. I'm Oakdale but my close friends call me Oakee, my brother calls me Oakhead and my mommie calls me Dale. Ya'll don't make me cry talking about my momma, I just started this story. I'm 23 years old and I'm from Houston Texas but i've been living in Portland Maine for the last seven months. Sometimes I say Portland "Mayne hold up" but people up here don't get it. Everyday I say I'm getting on a plane and never coming back. I mean they got crazy cheap deals on spirit airlines but some of them deals be too good to be true right? I work at Trader Joes 4 days a week in the evening and Hannaford Supermarket 5 days in the morning. but I do have 720 instagram followers. Don't be a hater because you stay on IG and you follow way more people than people who follow you and stay looking thirsty. I'm sorry we started off on the wrong foot. Anyways,  I look forward to getting out of work early on friday and go walking in old port with Sparrow. Sparrow is my roommate and ex fiance's sister. It's alot to unpack I know. long story short, I moved to Portland after Victor, my ex proposed to me over the phone after about a year of dating long distance. Just know that Facetime isn't face to face and it's 6 other days in the week after he post you as his WCW. I digress. My mom didn't know I was engaged or in a serious relationship for that matter. She thinks I go to the University of Southern Maine, even though I never stepped foot on campus. Listen, I'm figuring it all out. Which brings me to Fort Bloom.

    

        Fort Bloom is a place where women ROAR!!! Just imagine me yelling that in the lunch room at work as I often do. Fort Bloom isn't a place but an idea. Come to think of it, I guess it could be a place. How much does a small town cost? Basically it's an idea for now, where women can thrive and overcome whatever challenges that they may face. Where women can make there dreams become reality. That's where you come in. I'm reaching out to people online to help me figure this all out. that includes you. Yes you reading this right now. My story is your story.  You can contribute to the continuation of my life. that includes my friends and family. You can introduce new characters, and even choose were I decide to work and live in the future. You write the story. At the bottom there is a box where you can contribute. Once submitted, if your version of future events in the life of Oakdale is selected, it will be published to the storyline along with your name listed as the contributer. You can also submit drawings of characters or even short films of your version of events.  There will also at times be alternate version of events that will be highlighted at the same time that will be put to a vote as the official version. Please allow you mind to wonder.
